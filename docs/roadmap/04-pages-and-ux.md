# Pages and UX

## Pages
- `/`: cinematic landing page and project pitch.
- `/map`: full-screen live simulator.
- `/datasets`: data catalog and viewers.
- `/scenarios`: scenario browser and future editor.
- `/leaderboards`: human, agent, browser-native, SUMO, and SOTA rankings.
- `/papers`: technical papers and methodology.

## Visual Direction
- Keep the current dark cinematic transport-control-room mood.
- Avoid generic dashboards.
- Use glass panels, strong typography, glowing traffic layers, and map-first composition.
- Make `/map` feel like the product, not a secondary feature.
